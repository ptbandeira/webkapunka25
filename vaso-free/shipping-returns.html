Shipping & Returns

Shipping

Processing time: 1–2 business days.

Regions served: EU, UK, and select international destinations.

Carrier: tracked postal/courier partners.

Typical transit time: EU 2–5 business days; international 6–12 business days.

Returns

We accept unopened returns within 14 days of delivery.

How to start a return: email hello@kapunka.com with your order number.

Refunds are issued to the original payment method within 5–10 business days after we receive and inspect the return.
